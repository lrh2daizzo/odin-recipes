Copycat Ramen

Description

My version of the TikTok ramen recipe. Made with complex carbs and veggies!

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Fish Sauce
  • Napa Cabbage
  • Butter
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Everything Bagel Seasoning
  • Furikake

Steps

  1. Step 1:

    2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add ramen and cook for 1 minute, then use a fork to gently separate noodles. Reduce heat to a low boil and cook until noodles are just soft, about 3 minutes. Drain and set aside.

  2. Step 2:

    3. While ramen is cooking, mix soy sauce, sugar, and fish sauce together in a small bowl; set aside. Separate inner parts (cores) of the shredded napa cabbage from the outer parts (leaves).

  3. Step 3:

    4. Melt butter in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add carrots and cook for 1 1/2 minutes. Add shredded napa cores and garlic; cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Add shredded napa leaves and soy mixture; cook for 30 to 45 seconds. Add drained ramen and bean sprouts; cook until heated through, about 1 minute.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Transfer ramen mixture to a serving bowl. Add bagel seasoning and furikake; mix well.
